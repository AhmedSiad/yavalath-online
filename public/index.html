Yavalath Online

Yavalath is a computer-generated board game created by a program called Ludi which was made by Cameron Browne in 2007.

I could not find anywhere online to easily play this interesting board game online with friends, so I decided to make my own.

Rules

The rules of the game are simple: two players, white and black, alternate turns placing their pieces down on the hexagonal board. If a player makes a line of 3 of their pieces in a row, they lose the game. However, if they make a line of 4 or more of their pieces in a row, they win the game.
